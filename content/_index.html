I am a senior data scientist at Attest. I am also a member of the Quanteda Initiative and a mentor under the Research-to-Business Mentorship Scheme for LSE PhD candidates and post-docs looking to transition into data science.

As a computational social scientist, my core expertise lies in statistical analysis and predictive modelling. At Attest, I build algorithmic models for fraud detection to screen out high-risk respondents in surveys. In addition, I am developing a novel sampling framework to mitigate bias when multiple panels are blended in a single survey.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher in analytic software development at the London School of Economics. At the same time, I was a research scientist at Uppsala University working on the automation of the Uppsala Conflict Data Program (UCDP) using natural language processing (NLP) and active learning.
