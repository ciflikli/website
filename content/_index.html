I am a senior data scientist at Attest. I am also a member of the Quanteda Initiative and a guest lecturer for the LSE summer school course Introduction to Data Science and Machine Learning.

As a computational social scientist, my core expertise lies in

  • Latent variable analysis: using psychometric tools in R, modelling latent traits of individuals to obtain measurements

  • Predictive modelling: developing machine learning pipelines in Python with an emphasis on robust design and methodology to identify associations

  • Causal inference: conducting experiments utilising various types of interventions and computing counterfactuals to understand the why

Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher in analytic software development at the Department of Methodology at the London School of Economics. In addition, I was a research scientist at Uppsala University working on the automation of the Uppsala Conflict Data Program (UCDP) using natural language processing (NLP) and active learning.
